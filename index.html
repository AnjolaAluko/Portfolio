About Me

DIRECTOR | CINEMATOGRAPHER | EDITOR | GRAPHICS DESIGNER

I am an experienced filmmaker with over five years of expertise in cinematography, directing, video editing, and graphic design. I have a strong passion for storytelling and a sharp eye for detail, which helps me bring creative ideas to life through compelling visuals. As a graduate of the MultiChoice Talent Factory, I received specialized training in cinematography, directing, and video editing, gaining valuable skills and knowledge of the filmmaking process.

I am skilled at turning ideas into visually engaging stories that connect with different audiences. My experience spans feature films, series, documentaries, and commercials. I excel in managing production workflows, fostering collaboration among teams, and delivering high-quality projects on schedule and within budget.

My Achievement

MULTICHOICE TALENT FACTORY, WEST AFRICA

The MultiChoice Talent Factory (MTF) is a transformative program that equips African filmmakers with world-class training, practical experience, and industry connections.

I had the privilege of serving as the Director on one of the feature films, GONE during my time at the MultiChoice Talent Factory. I was honored as the 2nd-best graduating student, earning a two-week, all-expenses-paid internship on a Zee World production in Mumbai, India.

This incredible opportunity has enhanced my filmmaking skills and broadened my global perspective, reinforcing MTF's mission to empower African storytellers.
